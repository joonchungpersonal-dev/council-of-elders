## Notes on Acquisition

### Books (Digital vs Physical)
- **Kindle/eBooks**: Easier to process for text extraction
- **Physical**: Some rare books only available in print
- **Audible**: Could use transcription, but lower quality

### Legal Considerations
- Fair use allows quotation for educational purposes
- Full text ingestion may require licensing
- Summarization and key quotes are clearly permissible

### Alternative Sources
- **Libraries**: Many available through library digital lending
- **Archive.org**: Some older texts available
- **Author websites**: Often have free excerpts/articles
